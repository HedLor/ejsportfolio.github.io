Boolean Bot Battle

In this interactive game, bots battle using Boolean logic operations (AND, OR, XOR, NOT). Players can create and configure bots with unique names, Boolean operations, initial values (0 or 1), and speed settings. The bots are randomly placed in a 5x5 arena, where they move and collide. When two bots collide, their Boolean operation decides the winner, and the leaderboard dynamically tracks wins and losses.
